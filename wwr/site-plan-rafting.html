Overview

Purpose

The goal of this site is to bring you a fun, affordable and enjoyable rafting experience for young adults.

Audience

The target audience for this website is the young adults between 18 and 30 years old that are interested in spending a great time with their friends and taking a break from work or school. What do they want? They want to break the daily routine and have some fun with their family and friends to relax and have a good time together. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will find detailed information of the different adventures on trips that we offer, contact information and company information. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? They wil usually access through laptops and mobile devices.
